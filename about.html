About Me

Personal Statement

Hello there! I'm Navneet Nair. My journey in software is driven by a deep curiosity about how Computer systems work from the ground up, from the lowest transistor to the bulkiest OS. I began exprloring basic programming when I was 12, but only began my formal studies in Computer Science at IIIT Hyderabad while pursuing my Bachelors programme. A lot of my expertise lies in writing C and Python code, where I have built a variety of projects ranging from simple games to machine learning models. I also have a relatively strong understanding of Data Structures and Algorithms that allows me to write efficient code and solve complex problems.

Recently, I've been expanding my horizons into machine learning and data science. I had learnt the basic concepts and mathematics behind Linear and Logistic regression, Gradient Descent, and how these concepts scale up into Backpropagation in Large Neural Networks of all kinds of architectures. I also managed to implement a few small multilayer perceptrons using Tensorflow and train them on small, industry standard datasets. Of late, I have been exploring the various ideas of Computer Vision, and I have been experimenting with OpenCV to build some small projects in that domain.

My ultimate goal is to find new and exciting applications for cutting edge technology in generating value and improving society as a whole!
